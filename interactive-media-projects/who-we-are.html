Who We Are

See Demo See Code

I acted in this project as a Pixel Artist, Animator, Game Programmer, and Game Designer.

I utilized Aseprite to extend free art assets, and C# in Unity2D as a game engine.

ONE

Product

Who We Are is a Unity2D 2-player platformer game made during the CUNY 2019 Hackathon, for the theme of "Environment." Inspired by Nidhogg, it draws on steampunk and themes of nature as the narrative behind the competitive play.

The project was completed in a team of 6, with 1 other programmer, in a 2 day timeframe.

Key Features: Platformer physics, projectile mechanics

TWO

Process

I created all assets from scratch, which were eventually integrated into the game to replace placeholders. These were made using Aseprite. After finishing animations, backgrounds, and the title screen, I helped finish the HP system.

Some things I learned from the project are:

  • Projectiles
  • HP
  • Win condition

THREE

Outcome

The game is deployed on itch.io as a WebGL file.
